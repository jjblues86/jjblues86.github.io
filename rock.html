Here's a short list of restaurants that play rock music...

The Crocodile

Rock, Punk, Indie music

Serves Italian and American cuisine

Link to Website

The Crocodile Cafe is a musical venue renowned for its inclusion of local musicians as well as nationally touring acts. Despite a brief closure due to fire damage in 2007, the Crocodile has wowed Seattle crowds with acts such as R.E.M., Nirvana, Tom Morello, and more for twenty-five years.

Hard Rock Cafe

Rock and Pop music

Serves burgers, sandwiches, and barbeque

Link to Website

One of several restaurants in the esteemed Hard Rock Cafe chain, Hard Rock Cafe has been associated with rock music for decades. The specific Seattle location is not known for live acts, but features previously unreleased versions of famous tracks previously unheard of by the general public.

Rendezvous

Rock, Classical, and Big Band music

Small plates and classic American entrees

Link to Website

Located adjacent to the classic venue of the Jewel Box, Rendezvous offers drinks and small plates for those visiting for a show.
